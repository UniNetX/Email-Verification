Common Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and tap "Sign Up". You'll need to verify your email address before you can sign in.

How do I join a team?

Ask your team coach or captain to send you an invite code. Enter the code in the app to join the team.

I forgot my password

Tap "Forgot Password" on the sign-in screen. We'll send you a password reset link via email. This may only be done on a mobile device.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action cannot be undone, and you may not use the same email to register another account.
